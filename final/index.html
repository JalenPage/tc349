Delta Pi Chapter

Welcome to the official website of the Delta Pi Chapter of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc. We have been an influential entity at Michigan State University since 1956 and we continue to embody our fundamental purpose of achievement in every field of human endeavor. We are honored to serve the community in the greater Lansing and Detroit area, as we are The Pride of the Northern Province. We thank you for visiting our website, and we look forward to seeing you at our events and programs.
